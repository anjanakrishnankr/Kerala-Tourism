Vagamon

Vagamon is an Indian hill station and a revenue village primarily located in Peerumedu Taluk of Idukki district (majority area including Vagamon town), and also Meenachil taluk and Kanjirappally taluk of Kottayam district in the state of Kerala, India.Located in the Western Ghats 25 kilometres (16 mi) east of Erathupetta on the border of Kottayam-Idukki districts, Vagamon is famous for its natural beauty.The Vagamon glass bridge is the longest cantilever glass bridge in India.

Illikkal Kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a Thalanadu located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India.The distance from kottayam railway station to illikal kallu is 57km. Situated at around 3500 feet above sea level, Illickal Kallu is a major tourist attraction in Thalanadu.
